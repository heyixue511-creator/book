# Atrt Sinsabaug

Midwest Landscape #53 (1962) is among the 40 photographs by Art Sinsabaugh that will be on view in the third-floor Steichen Center through October 31. Selected by John Szarkowski, Director of the Department of Photography, ART SINSABAUGH: LANDSCAPES includes not only Sinsabaugh's photographic studies of the Midwest (both town and country) but also more recent work that was done in New England. Born in 1924, Sinsabaugh has long been artistically concerned with the American landscape and published a selection of views of Illinois in the book 6 Mid-America Chants/ll Midwest Landscapes, with text by Sherwood Anderson. His work has been seen recently in MIRRORS AND WINDOWS: AMERICAN PHOTOGRAPHY SINCE 1960.
