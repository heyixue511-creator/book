## 15 Satu Miettinen

Dr. Satu Miettinen works as a co-ordinator for the Competence Center of Industrial Design at the Savonia University of Applied Sciences. For several years she has been working with service design, and currently she is a research leader and director in the service design project called Service design for Elderly funded by the Finnish Funding Agency for Innovation and Technology. She also worked as a principal investigator in the “Experiencing

Well-being – Developing New User Interfaces and Service Platforms for Leisure” project, which was also funded by the Finnish Funding Agency for Innovation and Technology, TEKES. In the past she has worked as a project manager and specialist in the areas of crafts development, cultural and creative tourism in several international and European Union funded projects during the period 1997–2006.
