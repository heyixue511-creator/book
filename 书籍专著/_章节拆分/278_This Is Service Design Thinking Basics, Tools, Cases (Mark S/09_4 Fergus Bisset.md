## 4 Fergus Bisset

At the time of writing Fergus is a Research Assistant in the Human Centred Design Intitute in the School of Engineering and Design at Brunel University in West London, UK. He is developing a public engagement exhibition with The Design Museum, London titled “Ergonomics – Real Design”. A social media enthusiast, Fergus is also an associate with Research in Practice for Adults and Destinable. When not wearing his design hat, Fergus can be found wearing his ski hat or running shoes.
