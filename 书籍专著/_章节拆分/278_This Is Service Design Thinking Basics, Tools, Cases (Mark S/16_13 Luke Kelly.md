## 13 Luke Kelly

Luke Kelly graduated from the University of Liverpool in 2007, obtaining a Masters degree in Politics and the Mass Media. After working as a researcher for a UK politician, he spent a year living in Shanghai whilst lecturing at Xian-Jiaotong Liverpool University, before travelling to Amsterdam to work with STBY. Now based in STBY’s London office, Luke’s main interests concern the effect technology exerts on culture. He’s currently using the ethnographic experience gained from working within service design to write a PhD proposal examining the impact of techno-cultural media on the Japanese public sphere. He’s also woefully inept at playing football.
