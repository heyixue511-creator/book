Bricolage is the construction or creation of a work from a diverse range of things that happen to be available, or a work created by such a process. The term is borrowed from a French word referring to amateur repair and DIY maintenance work. Someone who practices bricolage is called a bricoleur. The term has been used in many other fields, including intellectual pursuits, education, computer software, and business. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bricolage.
