## Anne Massey

FRSA graduated in the History of Modern Art and Design at the University of Northumbria in 1980, and gained her doctorate there in 1985 with research on the Independent Group and post-war British design theory. She was awarded an MBA in 2ooo by the Open University. She has lectured and written extensively on the history of design and culture; her book The Independent Group: Modernism and Mass Culture in Britain, 1945-59 was published in 1996 and her most recent book, Hollywood Beyond the Screen: Design and Material Culture was published in 200o. She is currently Professor of Design History and Head of the Centre for Creative Management at Southampton Institute.
