## Bibliographical Note

This Dover edition, first published in 2006, is an unabridged republication of Goethe’s Theory of Colours, first published by John Murray, London, in 1840. The first German edition (entitled Zur Farbenlehre) was published by J.G. Cotta’schen Buchhandlung, Tübingen, in 1810.
