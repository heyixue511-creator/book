## The Transition Design Framework

Transition Design uses a heuristic model (Figure 1) to characterize four different but interrelated and mutually influencing areas: (1) vision; (2) theories of change; (3) mindset/posture; and (4) new ways of designing. We welcome suggestions on how to refine, expand, or evolve the framework and its four sections.
