## DRAWING

Drawing is a process. It is about representing an idea, or series of ideas, of how something will look or the format it will take, but it is also a form of visual thinking, a way to make decisions in the process of creation. For those skilled in sketching, the best way to draw is quickly, to get the idea down on paper and record it while it is still fresh in your head. You don’t have to be able to draw well to communicate an idea (stick men are fine), but the practice does help you to see better, and to notice things. The following exercise will help free your eye and your mind from habitual ways of seeing.
