Two Geometric Modernist typefaces. Futura (above) was designed in Germany in 1924-26 by Paul Renner. Memphis (below) was designed in 1929 by Rudolf Wolf, art director at the Stempel Foundry. The original design for Futura included text figures and many highly geometric, alternative characters which have never been issued in metal, though The Foundry (London) issued them in digital form in 1994.
