## 33 Elm Tree House, High Street,

Various unexecuted schemes for repairing Elm Tree   
House as a dwelling (19023); executed plans for the   
adaptation of Elm Tree House and the malthouse at   
the back as premises for the Campden School of Arts   
and Crafts (19o4); moving a small slated cottage to   
stand next to the malthouse (1907—8)   
Client: The Campden School of Arts and Crafts   
Drawings: CT   
Sources: Ashbee Collection, vol.I, pp. I34-S, 149, 15I, vol. 3 Cottages, pp. 1268 WGC, pp. 86-7 and platcs 78-85   
The malthouse has been demolished.
