Urban Geography of Jerusalem (Berlin and New York, 1973), pp. 140-4 suggests that Ashbee drew up a town plan in 1922 in collaboration with Patrick Geddes. This may be so, but no evidence is produced to support the idea, and I do not know of any. There is a draft zoning plan dated Jan 192I among Ashbee's papers, Box IV, Folder I, FA Jerusalem. It is annotated 'In this plan the MacLean and the Geddes plans have been co-ordinated'. This remark can be sufficiently explained by the fact that Ashbee's town plan drew on McLean's plan of 1918 and Geddes's of 1919.
