## 55 23-37 (odd) Squirrels Heath Avenue,

Gidea Park, Romford, Essex   
A group of new detached and semi-detached houses Date: 1910-11   
Contractor: R. Emmott   
Materials: brick   
Source: Gidea Park Exhibition Committee, The Book of the Exhibition of Houses and Cottages (I9II), pp.141-2   
These houses were designed in association with Gripper and Stevenson, architects. The scheme envisaged houses on both sides of Squirrels Heath Avenue, but they were built on one side only.
