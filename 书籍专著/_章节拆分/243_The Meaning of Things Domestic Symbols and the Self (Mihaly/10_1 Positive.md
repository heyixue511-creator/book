## 1 Positive

a Any description of the home that uses adjectives connoting a positive emotional tone; e.g., "warm; happy; fun; affirmative; cheery; cozy; special ambience; friendly; free." Do Not code "comfortable" here, nor positive descriptions of physical characteristics, i.e., "large; beautiful; warm" (when referring to temperature)

b Or any statement of positive affect toward the home, e.g., "I really like it; There are a lot of things in it I like; I love my home."
