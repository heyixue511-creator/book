To be sure, this is not quite so formidable as it sounds, because all such aims are interrelated; it would be impossible to pursue any one of them effectively without simultaneously (and, to an extent, quite automatically) pursuing the others. For this kind of planning, it is not enough for administrators in most fields to undersmend specifc services and techniques. They must understand, and understand thoroughly, specific places.
