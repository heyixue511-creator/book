At the outbreak of World War II, Trump was again called up into the army, served as a company commander, and was wounded in the stomach during the last days of the war. The Munich school had been almost completely destroyed by bombing in January 1944; rebuilding at a time of general shortages was slow; and the administration had to be reorganised as well.
