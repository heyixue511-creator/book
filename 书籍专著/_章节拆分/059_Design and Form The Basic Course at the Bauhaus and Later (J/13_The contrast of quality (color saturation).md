## The contrast of quality (color saturation):

This is the contrast between luminous and dull colors.   
Colors can be subdued by the addition of black, white.   
gray, or complementary colors.

The contrast of quantity:

This is based on the opposition of colored areas of different sizes.

It is necessary to study the world of colors generally, and as comprehensively as possible. The color star,he projec tion of the surface of the color sphere onto a plane with the gradations of the twelve main colors towards black and white, provides a good survey of the structure of the whole essence of color. I developed this color star in 1921. It formed the basis of my teaching of color at the Bauhaus.
