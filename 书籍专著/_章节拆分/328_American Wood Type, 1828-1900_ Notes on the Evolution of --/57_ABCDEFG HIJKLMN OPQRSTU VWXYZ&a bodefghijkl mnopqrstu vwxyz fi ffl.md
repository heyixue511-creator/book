This cutting came from a country newspaper office in central Nebraska.The owner remembered his father telling of purchasing the type from salvage of a well-established newspaper shop in South Dakota that had burned in 1885. This type appears to have been carved entirely by band.The Roman Extended shown

was also purchased from the same source, but it was a machine-made type.
