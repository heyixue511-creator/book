## Chapter 2 : Flowers and trees

Camellias: Sichuan camellias (Camellia japonica) and Yunnan camellias (Camellia reticulata) are both valuable, and the yellow ones are particularly hard to obtain . People often match them together with magnolia, since they bloom at the same season, and the white and red make a dazzling show. This is rather vulgar. There is another variety called 'Drunken imperial concubine' (Camellia japonica var. anemoniflora) which opens when the snow is on the ground and is particularly attractive.2
