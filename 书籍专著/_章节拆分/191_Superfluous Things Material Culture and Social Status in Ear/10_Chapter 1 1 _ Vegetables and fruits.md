## Chapter 1 1 : Vegetables and fruits

Mandarin oranges: Mandarin oranges from the region of Lake Dongting are particularly sweet, while those from Xinzhuang have no juice and need to be peeled with a knife before eating. There is another variety with an even thicker skin, called miluo mandarin oranges which are particularly fine. The little ones are called 'golden mandarins', and the round ones 'golden beans'. 12
