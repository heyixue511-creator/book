(October 1929-March 1930) Architecture Era, as the name translates, was published by Kuseishashob, Tokyo. The founding editor, Koike Shinji (190181), was a major design critic, and despite the short life (five issues) of this periodical it furthered the concept of the so-called International Style in architecture in Japan. Issue number 2, November 1929, was the Bauhaus issue, cover designed by Horiguchi Sutemi (1895-1984). The periodical measures 30 by 22 cm.
