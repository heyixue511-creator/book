## 1 Saishin no shyashin chishiki

The vanguard character of the covers of Asahi Camera (1926-1942, 1949-to date). the name under which the magazine is more commonly known, was limited mainly to the period from the early to perhaps the late 1930s. The periodical was published in both Tokyo and Osaka and was edited from 1934 to 1938 by Hoshino Tatsuo. For the cover shown, from an issue published for the amateur as well as the specialist, the designer is unknown,

The periodical measures 26.3 by 19 cm.
