## 12. Shiseido Gurafu

(June 1930-September 1937)

This one of several house organs of Shiseido Cosmetic Company was edited by Shinohara Masao. This first issue shows the use of photomontage in its internal layout. The two-page spread features an article on Greta Garbo's eyes. The use of photography by Shiseido in its publications was to a great degree the work of Ibuka Akira and the designer Maeda Mitsugu. Ibuka was a member of the circle around Photo Times. Both men were primarily responsible for introducing photomontage to Shiseido advertising program.

The periodical measures l9 by l3 cm.

## . Nippon Gekijo News

(Number l2, 1934)

Back cover advertisement for Kurabu Cream Toothpaste. The text boasts, among other things, "The fragrance is modern" and "Number One in the Toothpaste World." This periodical measures 20.6 by l4.8 cm.
