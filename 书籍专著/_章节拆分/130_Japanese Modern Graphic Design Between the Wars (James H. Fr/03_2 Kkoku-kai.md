## 2 Kkoku-kai

(1926-4l)

"Advertising World" was the single most important advertising periodical bringing Modernist trends to the attention of Japanese designers throughout the 1920s and 1930s. Its founding editor, Murota Kuraz, was a major editorial influence in Japan in his time as well being a graphic designer of some note. (His Shten zuan sensy portfolio is described in the preceding chapter on Western design guides.) Throughout its history Kkoku-kai published contributions from important critics and designers.

The dates of the covers shown here are:   
a. February 1933   
bApril 1933   
c. June 1933   
d. September 1933   
The periodical measures 25.6 by 19 cm.
