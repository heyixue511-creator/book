## 3. Creative control

In traditional photography, the composition of the shot and the choice of lighting happens in the camera, but there is another set of opportunities for creative control that happens later in processing and printing. The team realized that digital photography is not so sequential. Any time during the process, you can apply filters, pixilate, choose sepia, add picture frames, text, and so on. This realization led to this framework, expressed as five balloons around the user, rather than a linear journey through the experience.
