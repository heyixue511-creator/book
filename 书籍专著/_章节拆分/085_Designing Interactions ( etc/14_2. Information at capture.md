## 2. Information at capture

The real-time feedback of the screen on the back of the camera would emerge as a highly valued feature of the digital camera, so you could see immediately if the shot looked promising or disappointing. Information about when a picture was taken could also be recorded, along with the technical details of the image, and perhaps a voice annotation. In a future where GPS is inexpensive enough to be integrated into the camera, the information about where the shot was taken could make browsing and sorting a lot easier.
