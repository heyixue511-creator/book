## 1. Readiness to capture

The professional photographer travels with cases full of lenses, camera bodies, tripods, and lighting equipment. The team predicted that when digital photography is adopted by the general public, cameras will include a broad range of devices, from the traditional professional kit at the top end, through simple cameras with builtin lenses, to devices such as cell phones, or wearable cameras that would look like jewelry.
