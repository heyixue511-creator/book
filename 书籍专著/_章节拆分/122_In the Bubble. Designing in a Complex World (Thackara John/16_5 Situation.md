22. Ibid. For its project to develop a pier in Genoa, Italy, UN Studio transformed a twenty-three-thousand-square-mile harbor pier into a three-dimensional piazza. The design uses time-based planning represented diagrammatically as a circle of experience. Programs in the piazza are organized clockwise around activities clustered on the basis of views, time of day, and time of year. Coffee can be consumed in the morning sun with a view toward the sea; midday shopping offers shadow; evenings are spent watching the sunset.
