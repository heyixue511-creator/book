The Regionmaker decomposes a city’s environment into functional units (housing, services, commercial, industry ) and then runs through millions of combinations and suggests the optimal combination according to a given input of desires. The ‘‘access optimizer’’ function, for example, determines the physical structure of the city based on such parameters as preferred time expenditure, transportation means used, level of sustainability, economic efficiency, and ecological control.
