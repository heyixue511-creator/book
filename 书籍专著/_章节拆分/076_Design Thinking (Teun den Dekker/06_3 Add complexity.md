## 3 Add complexity:

During the repeated replay of the script, things come to mind that have not been thought of before.

Example of ‘reducing complaints’: What if all the complaint forms were used up? What happens if we add more passers-by?

 Also, try to get more abstract things on the table.

Example of reducing complaints: how long must the customer with a complaint wait for a response? What happens if the seller resolves the complaint himself immediately?
