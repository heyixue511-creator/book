1 “Chaussée” (in French) is the same as “Shosse” (in Russian) or “highway” (in English). To explain what Rublevskoe Chaussée is, I will offer an anecdote about an art critic whose car breaks down while he’s on the Rublevskoe Chaussée, leading to many villas inhabited by the nouveaux riches. As he hitchhikes at the side of the road, a car pulls up and the driver asks, “Are you in the oil business?” “No,” says the critic. And the driver zooms off.
