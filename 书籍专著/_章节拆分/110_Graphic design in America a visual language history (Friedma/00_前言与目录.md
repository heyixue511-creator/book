This book is published on the occasion of the inauguration of the exhibition Graphic Design in America: A Visual Language History, organized by Walker Art Center in collaboration with the American Institute of Graphic Arts. Following its premiere at the Walker Art Center, the exhibition will be shown at the IBM Gallery of Science and Art, New York; the Phoenix Art Museum, Phoenix, Arizona; and the Design Museum, Butlers Wharf, London.
