Even a brief review of this campaign, such as this essay offers, can be rewarding for design historians and practitioners. A case study of the design activity sponsored by the American aluminum industry affords historians the opportunity to explicate the complex relationship between design and industry in the modern era. A reviw of the place and role of design in the American aluminum industry also challenges historians to distinguish between the emerging field of design history and other scholarly disciplines such as the history of technology or business studies.
